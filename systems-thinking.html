Systems Thinking Toolkit

Causal Loop Diagram

A Causal Loop Diagram (CLD) is a visual tool used in systems thinking to represent the feedback loops and cause-and-effect relationships within a system. It helps identify how different variables influence each other over time, revealing reinforcing and balancing loops that drive system behavior.

In this project, I created a Causal Loop Diagram to map out the key dynamics influencing student engagement in engineering projects. For example, increased team collaboration leads to higher project motivation, which boosts productivity and in turn fosters more collaboration—a reinforcing loop. Conversely, a lack of clarity in task assignments can lead to confusion and delays, reducing motivation—a balancing loop.

By analyzing this diagram, I was able to identify leverage points to improve team performance and overall project outcomes.
